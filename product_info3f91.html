Additional Charges:


 

 

 

Payment Term

A compulsory deposit of NT500 (+NT60 processing fee) is collected upon reservation. The deposit can be paid by credit card or through PayPal account. The deposit (NT500) will be deducted from your service fee whereas the rest of the fees can be paid in cash to the driver at the end of your journey each day.

For credit card/PayPal payment in full amount, there will be a 6% processing fee of the total amount. Payment must be made at least 24 hours before the booking departure time.

 

Cancellation

The deposit will not be refunded in case of cancellations. 

However, if the cancellation is made within 48 hours before the booking departure time, the deposit may be applied to future trips.
