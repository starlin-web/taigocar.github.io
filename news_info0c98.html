Taipei City Tour

Visiting top attractions in Taipei city

  1. Share：

Taipei City Day Tour 

Pick up/Drop off: Taipei City 

Itinerary: Chiang Kai Shek Memorial Hall, Presidential Office Building, Taipei 101 Observatory, Elephant Mountain, Ximending Market

Departure date and time: At your own choice (Please message us to check for availability)

*** Itinerary, pickup/drop off location may be amended according to needs. Please feel free to contact us. ***

 

Chiang Kai Shek Memorial Hall - A famous national monument built in memory of Generalissimo Chiang Kai-shek, former President of the Republic of China 

 

Presidential Office Building - Workplace of the president of R.O.C designed by architect Uheiji Nagano during the period of Japanese rule

 

Taipei 101 Observatory - The observation deck on the 89th floor offer a 360-degree city view

 

Elephant Mountain - Hiking trail that offers the best view of the Taipei 101

 

Ximending Market - Offers a variety of street food and fashion clothing shops
